Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Shramic's services, technology, and how we support farmers.

General Questions

What is Shramic and what services do you provide?

Shramic is an agricultural technology platform that bridges traditional farming with modern innovation. We provide smart farming solutions including IoT sensors for crop monitoring, AI-powered crop advisory, weather forecasting, market intelligence, direct market linkages, and comprehensive farmer training programs. Our goal is to help farmers increase productivity while ensuring sustainability.

How can Shramic help improve my farm's productivity?

Our platform combines real-time data monitoring, AI-driven insights, and expert agricultural knowledge to optimize every aspect of farming. From precision irrigation recommendations to pest detection and market timing, we help you make data-driven decisions that can increase yields by up to 30% while reducing resource waste.

Is Shramic suitable for small-scale farmers?

Absolutely! We believe that every farmer deserves access to modern agricultural tools, regardless of farm size. Our solutions are scalable and affordable, with flexible pricing options designed specifically for small and marginal farmers. We also offer training and support in regional languages to ensure accessibility.

Technology & Services

What technology do I need to use Shramic's services?

All you need is a smartphone with internet connectivity. Our mobile app works on both Android and iOS devices and is designed to be user-friendly, even for those with limited technical experience. For advanced features like soil sensors, we provide all necessary equipment and installation support.

How accurate are your weather forecasts and crop advisories?

Our weather forecasts use advanced meteorological models with over 85% accuracy for 7-day predictions. Crop advisories are generated by combining AI analysis with inputs from experienced agronomists, ensuring recommendations are both data-driven and practically validated. We continuously improve our models based on local conditions and farmer feedback.

Do you provide support in regional languages?

Yes! Our platform supports multiple Indian languages including Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, Marathi, Bengali, and more. Our customer support team is also available in regional languages to ensure you get help in the language you're most comfortable with.
