About Shramic Networks

We are building more than a company — we are building hope, dignity, and opportunity for rural India.
Shramic Networks Private Limited is a rural-first technology organization dedicated to empowering farmers, workers, and entrepreneurs by connecting them with meaningful work, trusted experts, and essential services — all through simple, accessible digital platforms.

Our journey is rooted in one belief:
no one should be left behind because of where they live.

Our Story

Shramic Networks was born from listening — listening to farmers searching for reliable workers, to skilled laborers waiting months for work, to rural youth full of talent but limited by access.

We saw a painful gap between skills and opportunities, between knowledge and those who need it most. While technology advanced rapidly, rural communities were often forced to adapt instead of being truly included.

In September, Shramic Networks began with a single purpose — to bridge this gap.

What started as an agriculture-focused idea soon grew into a larger mission. We realized that employment, knowledge, livestock, construction, and rural businesses are deeply connected. To create real change, we had to build an ecosystem, not just an app.

Today, Shramic Networks stands as a growing Shramic Community — a connected digital space where farmers, workers, experts, and service providers come together to support one another, grow together, and move forward with confidence.

"Technology should serve people — not the other way around."

Our Mission

To empower rural communities by creating a trusted digital ecosystem that connects people to employment, expert guidance, and essential services — enabling sustainable income, skill growth, and dignity of work.

Our Vision

We envision a future where rural India is not dependent, but digitally empowered, skilled, and self-reliant — where agriculture, construction, and livestock sectors thrive through collaboration, innovation, and community trust.

Our Core Values

Farmer-First

Every decision we make begins with understanding the real lives, struggles, and aspirations of rural people.

Purpose-Driven Innovation

We innovate not for complexity, but for impact, simplicity, and usability.

Trust & Transparency

We believe trust is built through fairness, verification, and accountability — especially where livelihoods are involved.

Sustainability

True growth protects both people and the land for generations to come.

Community Before Company

Shramic Networks grows only when the Shramic Community grows.
