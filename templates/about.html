About Shramic Networks

We're on a mission to transform agriculture through innovation, empowering farmers with the tools and knowledge they need to thrive in the modern world.

Our Story

Shramic Networks was born from a simple observation: while technology was transforming every industry, agriculture—the backbone of our economy—was being left behind. Our founders, coming from both agricultural and technology backgrounds, saw firsthand the challenges farmers faced daily.

In 2020, we started with a vision to bridge this gap. What began as a small pilot project with 100 farmers in Karnataka has now grown into a movement serving over 50,000 farmers across India. Our journey has been driven by one core belief: that every farmer deserves access to the best tools and knowledge, regardless of their location or resources.

"Technology should empower, not intimidate. We build solutions that are powerful yet simple, accessible yet affordable."

Today, we're not just a technology company—we're partners in the success of thousands of farming families. Every feature we build, every training program we conduct, and every innovation we introduce is designed with real farmers' needs in mind.

Our Mission

To empower every farmer with cutting-edge technology and knowledge, making sustainable and profitable farming accessible to all. We strive to increase agricultural productivity while preserving our environment for future generations.

Our Vision

A future where technology and tradition work hand-in-hand, where every farmer has the tools to succeed, and where agriculture becomes a proud, prosperous, and sustainable profession for generations to come.

Our Core Values

Farmer-First

Every decision we make starts with asking: "How does this help our farmers?" Their success is our success.

Innovation

We constantly push boundaries to bring the latest agricultural technology within reach of every farmer.

Sustainability

Profitable farming must go hand-in-hand with environmental stewardship for future generations.

Integrity

We build trust through transparency, fair practices, and unwavering commitment to our farmers' welfare.
