The Shramic Vision: A Unified Rural Future

India’s heartland has always been the backbone of the nation, yet it has faced systemic fragmentation for decades. A farmer in rural Karnataka might have the best soil but no access to skilled harvesters. A construction worker in a growing town might have the skills but no way to verify them to a large contractor. Shramic Networks was built to solve exactly these "missing links."

Connecting the Dots

Our vision is simple yet profound: creating a digital ecosystem where agriculture, construction, and livestock sectors don’t exist in isolation, but support each other. Through our five specialized platforms—Krushi, Build, PashuSetu, Experts, and Business—we have created a loop of economic empowerment.

  • Skilled Livelihoods: We ensure that a worker's reputation follows them digitally, ensuring better pay and consistent work.
  • Expert Democratization: No farmer is too small to receive advice from a world-class agronomist.
  • Transparent Commerce: By removing the middleman, we put the profit back into the hands of the producer.

As we move into 2026, the Shramic Community is expanding. We are not just building apps; we are building dignity for the millions of unorganized workers and farmers across India. Technology, when applied with local context, becomes a superpower.

Precision Farming for the Marginal Farmer

The term "Precision Agriculture" often conjures images of giant self-driving tractors and million-dollar satellite arrays. For a typical Indian farmer with 2 acres of land, this feels like science fiction. However, Shramic Krushi is proving that precision farming is more about data than it is about expensive hardware.

The IoT Revolution in Your Pocket

We have introduced low-cost, solar-powered IoT sensors that monitor the three most critical parameters: Soil Moisture, N-P-K levels, and micro-climate temperature. This data is sent directly to the Shramic App in the farmer's regional language.

The Impact is Immediate:

  • Water Conservation: Farmers reduce water usage by 30% by only irrigating when the soil actually needs it.
  • Fertilizer Efficiency: Instead of broad-spectrum application, farmers apply exactly what the soil lacks, reducing costs.
  • Early Warning: Pest outbreaks often leave digital footprints in temperature changes. Our AI alerts the farmer before the first leaf turns yellow.

By democratizing this technology, we are ensuring that the small farmer isn't left behind in the global ag-tech race. Our goal is to double the income of 1 million farmers by 2027 through these small but powerful tech interventions.

Organizing Rural Construction Workforce

Urbanization is no longer just a "Big City" phenomenon. Tier-2 and Tier-3 cities in India are currently seeing the highest growth in residential and infrastructure construction. Yet, the labor market remains 90% unorganized. Skilled masons, electricians, and carpenters still have to wait at "labor chowks" every morning for a day's work.

The Shramic Build Solution

Shramic Build acts as a digital verification and discovery engine. We give every worker a "Digital Career Card" that records their skills, certifications, and work history verified by previous employers.

  • Trust for Contractors: Builders can hire teams with proven track records, reducing project delays.
  • Dignity for Workers: No more waiting on street corners. Workers receive job notifications on their phones based on their location and skill set.
  • Insurance and Safety: By organizing the workforce, we have been able to provide group insurance and safety training to thousands of workers who previously had no social safety net.

When a worker is verified and organized, their value in the market increases. Shramic Build is turning daily wage labor into a respected professional career path.

Livestock Digitization with PashuSetu

Livestock is the hidden wealth of rural India. It is a 365-day source of income that provides a safety net when crops fail. However, the livestock market is plagued by opaque pricing, lack of veterinary history, and unscientific breeding practices.

Enter PashuSetu

PashuSetu is a comprehensive management and commerce platform for livestock owners. It’s not just a marketplace; it’s a lifecycle support system.

Key Pillars of PashuSetu:

  • Medical Records: Digital tracking of vaccinations and health history, making the animal more valuable in the trade market.
  • Marketplace: A transparent buy-sell platform where price is determined by the animal's productivity and health records, not just appearance.
  • Fodder & Feed: Connecting owners with certified feed suppliers to ensure optimal milk and meat yields.

By treating livestock as a serious asset class, we are helping rural families build long-term wealth that isn't solely dependent on the monsoon.

The Expert Economy in Rural Growth

One of the biggest hurdles in rural development is the "Expert Access Gap." A farmer in a remote village shouldn't have to travel 100km to talk to an agronomist about a rare crop disease. Similarly, a dairy owner should have instant access to a veterinary consultant.

Shramic Experts: Consultation at a Click

We have onboarded over 500 verified specialists—from retired agricultural scientists to modern veterinary experts and financial consultants. Our platform allows for seamless video and voice consultations in regional dialects.

Real-World Impact:

  • Language No Bar: Our experts are filtered by the languages they speak, ensuring clear communication.
  • Voice-First: Many of our users prefer voice notes over text. Our system handles asynchronous voice chat with ease.
  • Affordability: By aggregating demand, we have reduced the cost of an expert consultation by 60%, making high-level advice accessible to everyone.

Knowledge is power, but only if it reaches the person at the right time. Shramic Experts ensures that the right advice is always just a phone call away.

Rural B2B: Building Strong Markets

For a rural economy to thrive, it needs a healthy business ecosystem. Shramic Business is our answer to the fragmented rural supply chain. Whether it's a seed nursery, an equipment rental shop, or a poultry supplier, they all need a way to reach the right customers efficiently.

The Marketplace Advantage

Shramic Business provides a transparent platform for B2B transactions. We connect bulk suppliers with groups of farmers (FPOs) and construction suppliers with small-scale contractors.

  • Bulk Purchase Savings: Groups of farmers can leverage the platform to buy seeds and fertilizers at wholesale rates.
  • Equipment Monetization: If a farmer owns a smart tractor, they can list it on Shramic Business to rent it out during downtime, creating extra income.
  • Inventory Tracking: We help small rural businesses manage their digital presence and inventory for the first time.

When rural businesses grow, they create local jobs. Shramic Business is the engine that keeps the rural economy spinning independently.
