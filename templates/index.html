Empowering Rural India Through Technology

Transforming agriculture, construction, and livestock livelihoods by connecting workers, farmers, experts, and businesses on a single digital ecosystem — the Shramic Community.

Shramic Networks builds inclusive platforms that create jobs, improve productivity, and deliver trusted services to India's unorganised workforce.

What We Do

At Shramic Networks, we build a unified digital ecosystem for rural India.
Our mission is to create employment, enable services, and deliver expert knowledge across agriculture, construction, and livestock sectors—empowering farmers, workers, and rural entrepreneurs through accessible, technology-driven platforms.

  • Smart Rural Employment & Services

    We connect farmers, workers, contractors, and service providers through digital platforms that simplify hiring, job discovery, equipment booking, and service access across agriculture, construction, and livestock sectors.

  • Skill Development & Expert Support

    We enable rural users to learn, upskill, and make informed decisions through verified experts, structured guidance, consultations, and knowledge resources—available in regional languages and voice-based formats.

  • Market Linkages & Rural Commerce

    We bridge rural demand and supply by enabling transparent market access, livestock trade, B2B services, nurseries, suppliers, and service providers—ensuring fair pricing, trust, and sustainable rural commerce.

Growing Together with Farmers

We believe in sustainable growth that benefits everyone in the agricultural ecosystem. From soil health to market access, we provide end-to-end support to help farmers thrive in the modern agricultural landscape.

Why Choose Shramic?

Shramic Networks Private Limited is a purpose-driven technology company dedicated to building a unified digital ecosystem for rural India. We design and operate integrated platforms that address critical challenges in employment generation, service accessibility, knowledge dissemination, and market connectivity across agriculture, construction, and livestock sectors.
By combining on-ground insights with scalable digital infrastructure, we enable rural communities to engage confidently and productively in the modern economy.

  • Technology That Works

    Shramic’s mobile and web platforms are designed for reliability, accessibility, and real-world usability in rural environments. Our solutions emphasize simplicity, multilingual support, and optimized performance for low-bandwidth conditions, ensuring they remain practical and inclusive across regions and user profiles.

    Through our integrated digital ecosystem, users can:

    • Discover and offer local employment opportunities
    • Hire verified workers and trusted service providers
    • Access professional consultations and expert guidance
    • Book equipment, services, and livestock-related resources
    • Manage work records, payments, and service history efficiently

    By interconnecting Shramic Krushi, Shramic Build, PashuSetu, Shramic Business, and Shramic Experts, we deliver a seamless unified platform where employment, services, knowledge, and commerce work together effortlessly.

  • Sustainable & Affordable

    We are committed to building solutions that are economically accessible and operationally sustainable for rural communities. Our platforms are designed to deliver long-term value while remaining affordable, transparent, and easy to adopt.

    • Clear and transparent pricing with fair service models
    • Affordable access for farmers, workers, and small enterprises
    • Verified users and professionals to ensure trust and accountability
    • Strong focus on long-term livelihood creation and stability

    Shramic Networks focuses on creating lasting impact by strengthening rural ecosystems, improving income resilience, and enabling informed decision-making through technology.

Our Commitment

At Shramic, we believe technology should simplify access to opportunity — not create complexity. Our commitment is to empower rural communities with reliable, inclusive systems designed thoughtfully for long-term social and economic impact.

  • Reliable platforms built for real-world rural conditions

  • Inclusive design with multilingual and voice-first experiences

  • Long-term social and economic impact for rural livelihoods
